How to reduce your Carbon footprint.

Home energy reduction techniques

  • Turn up thermostat in summer and turn down heating in winter.
    • Even modest changes can save over $30.00 a year and cut your CO2 emission by over 400 lbs!
  • Replace incandescent lightbulbs with energy star lights.
    • Can save $20.00 a year and 150 lbs of CO2.
  • Use cold water in your washer machine rather than hot water, and hang dry rather than using the dryer.
    • Can save $100.00 a year and 700 lbs of CO2.
  • Replace your refridgerator with a newer, energy efficient model.
    • Can save $100.00 a year and 700 lbs of CO2.
  • Replace Windows with new, more energy efficient panes.
    • Can save more than $150.00, and 2,947 lbs of CO2 a year.
